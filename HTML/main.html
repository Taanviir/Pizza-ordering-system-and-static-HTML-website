The History


The United Arab Emirates (or UAE for short) is a constitutional monarchy that is formed from a federation of seven emirates. The seven emirates include the capital Abu Dhabi, Dubai, Sharjah, Ajman, Ras Al Khaimah, Umm Al Quwain and Fujairah. The UAE declared independence on the 2nd of December, 1971 under the leadership of His Highness Sheikh Zayed bin Sultan Al Nahyan.

The Culture


The UAE culture characteristics are rooted from the Arab and Islamic heritage hospitality, tolerance, family cohesion and solidarity among members of society, as well as honor and pride. The City of Al Ain in Abu Dhabi emirate have been recognized as a heritage site in the UNESCO because it includes oases and some other important sites like Hili and Hafeet Mountains. Also, the emirate of Sharjah has been given the title of “Cultural Capital of the Arab World” in 2014, and that was because of the big contributions of the emirate to highlight the importance of the Arabic culture.

The Vision


After the Success the UAE had in the past couple of years with the first Emirati astronaut to go the space in 2019, and the first mission to mars through the explorer spacecraft that called the Hope lunched in 2020. The UAE does not plan to stop here, and these are the most important future projects:

  • Dubai plans to transforme 25% of the public transportation to Autonomous cars by year 2030.
  • Dubai plans to expand the use 3D printing for houses by year 2030, after the initial success in 2016.
  • The UAE plans for the first human settlement on mars by year 2117.
