Leaders in the UAE

The Founder: H.H Sheikh Zayed bin Sultan Al Nahyan


The Founder of the UAE and once the Ruler of Abu Dhabi, His Highness Sheikh Zayed bin Sultan Al Nahyan was a visionary leader who sought to unite the country by having all seven emirates combine to form the United Arab Emirates. One of His Highness’ earliest contributions came when he succeeded in helping Al Ain progress by establishing basic administration, personally funding the first modern school in the emirate of Abu Dhabi and convincing family members in assisting with smaller projects. One of the key foundations of his philosophy as a leader was that the resources of the country should be fully used to the benefit of the people, including the women of the UAE who benefited of his vision of education, employment, and equality.

The President (Abu Dhabi Ruler): H.H Sheikh Mohamed bin Zayed Al Nahyan


The 3rd and current President of the UAE and Ruler of Abu Dhabi, His Highness Sheikh Mohamed bin Zayed Al Nahyan became the president on the 14th of May 2022 after the passing of the previous President His Highness Sheikh Khalifa bin Zayed Al Nahyan. His Highness is known for being the driving force behind many initiatives in the country which boosted the security, socio-economic growth, and cultural diversity. In terms of economic development, His Highness contributed to the economic growth of the emirate of Abu Dhabi. His Highness was also instrumental in establishing the Environment Agency and even directed the Emirates Red Crescent to provide a helping hand to people afflicted by wars or disasters and crises.

Vice President-Prime Minister (Dubai Ruler): H.H Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum


The Vice-President and Prime Minister of the UAE and the Ruler of Dubai and Minister of Defense of the UAE, His Highness Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum helped propel Dubai to be one of the world’s leading cities in terms of urban development and tourism. He launched ground-breaking initiatives in a record time such as Vision 2021, the UAE National Agenda, the UAE Government Strategy Plan and much more. His Highness is also described as “one of the world’s most prominent real estate developers”. His Highness also oversaw numerous projects in Dubai such as the Dubai Internet City, Dubai Media City, the Dubai International Finance Center, Burj Al Arab and even drove the construction of the Burj Khalifa.

Member of the Supreme Council (Sharjah Ruler): H.H Sheikh Dr. Sultan bin Muhammad Al Qasimi


A member of the UAE’s Federal Supreme council and the Ruler of Sharjah, His Highness Sheikh Dr Sultan bin Muhammad Al Qasimi is an honorable man who has gained numerous honors and awards from numerous universities throughout the world. His Highness had completed two PhD’s, one in history at the Exeter University and another in political geography of the Gulf at Durham University. In addition to those, His Highness is also the President of the University of Sharjah and the American University of Sharjah.

Member of the Supreme Council (Ajman Ruler): H.H Sheikh Humaid bin Rashid Al Nuaimi


A member of the UAE’s Federal Supreme council and the Ruler of Ajman, His Highness Sheikh Humaid Bin Rashid Al Nuaimi III is one of the longest-reigning rulers in the world and under his reign, Ajman witnessed a number of major achievements in terms of vital projects. Thanks to His Highness’ contribution the foundations for a strong and prosperous economy has been grounded from the establishment of the Ajman Port and Ajman Free Zone.

Member of the Supreme Council (Umm Al Quwain Ruler): H.H Sheikh Saud bin Rashid Al Mualla


A member of the UAE’s Federal Supreme council and the Ruler of Umm Al Quwain, His Highness Sheikh Saud Bin Rashid Al Mualla was the Commanderof the Umm Al Quwain National Guard and had assisted his father in managing the emirate's affairs, oversaw many investment projects and established numerous government entities and local enterprises.

Member of the Supreme Council (Ras Al Khaimah Ruler): H.H Sheikh Saud bin Saqr Al Qasimi


A member of the UAE’s Federal Supreme council and the Ruler of Ras Al Khaimah, His Highness Sheikh Saud Bin Saqr Al Qasimi has helped Ras Al Khaimah establish itself as a robust and growing market with a diversified economic base. His Highness also helped establish many different Ras Al Khaimah based companies like RAK Ceramics, American University of the Ras Al Khaimah, the Ras Al Khaimah Centre for Advanced Materials and many more.

Member of the Supreme Council (Fujairah Ruler): H.H Sheikh Hamad bin Mohammed Al Sharqi


A member of the UAE’s Federal Supreme council and the Ruler of Fujairah, His Highness Sheikh Hamad bin Mohammed Al Sharqi served many roles before his before becoming the Ruler of Fujairah, some of which included the Chief of Police and Security in Fujairah. His Highness was also very keen on education and used to visit students and teachers to listen to their aspirations and find out their needs. Following his instructions, the Fujairah National theatre was established on the 4th of June 2006 which succeeded through local, Gulf and Arab performances.
